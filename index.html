Improbable Beef is looking to improve the taste of their product by finding bacteria that will take the flavor of the artificial meat to the next level! Volunteers who were interested in providing their own bacteria were asked to swab their belly buttons, provide demographic data, the type of belly button (i.e., innie "I" or outie "O"), and the frequency of washing their belly button per week.
